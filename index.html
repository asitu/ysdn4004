Interactive Dance Studio pop-up game aims to promote various cultural dance genres in the multicultural city of Toronto. Adults and children of all ages can experience a taste of dance sessions and learn fun choreography as a creative outlet for individuals.

Gather your friends and spot a IDS pop-up in selected locations in Toronto.
Download the mobile application and dress comfortably to dance!

Explore ten different cultural dances

Learn about cultural dances and thier history/origin

Start the gameplay

Compete with your friends and family and dance as a group or solo play. Select the type of dances that you want to learn and your preferred difficulty level.

Record your gameplay

Keep a memory of your experience by recording your dancing!

Earn points by aiming the correct moves

Use your phone or connect to Apple Watch as your controller and aim to perfect every move in the dance choreography.

Redeem points for free dance classes at local dance studios in Toronto. Continue your experience and learning with the dance community!
